About Me

I’m an ardent Nature lover and a self-taught photographer from India, specializing in Wildlife,Nature,street,creative and travel Photography.Won prizes and participated in more competitions. My photos have been embellished in all my social media. It is my intention to present – through the medium of photography – intuitive observations of the natural world which may have meaning to the spectators. For me, composition, self-satisfaction and photos that convey a story are imperative.What I like about photographs is that they capture a moment that’s gone forever, impossible to reproduce.

As a filmmaker I love to visualize more things which are un-imagine fact but some things like photography make it possible in real time by capturing the unexpected moments. I have started it by taking with mobile in college time that driven me till now with camera. Even though i have done short film, story writing,editing and all other filmMaking stuff when i take my camera go for shoot i make fresh and give me a power of freezing a moments. Since 2017 I have been doing it as a passion till now and it will not stop. I also teach photography in the field by giving tips in social media and also with people who reach me out.

Of course, I do post-process all of my images to retain their originality. I love playing with the image to bring out its artistic features,if the picture demands.

As of now I use a Nikon D5100 camera and Adobe Lightroom CC in Mobile for post-processing.

A photograph has the power to trap the attention of the viewer and lets them ponder for at least 10 seconds. Getting such a photograph can only happen if it contains a story behind it. And this will be my tip too ! Photograph a Story telling image consisting of unique composition and idea !
